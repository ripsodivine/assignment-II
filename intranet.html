Intranet

It is a private, internal network belonging to an organization(corporation, school, government).

It is used to share information, resources, and services among employees or members of the organization.

It uses the same technologies as the public Internet (TCP/IP, HTTP, web browsers, email) but is isolated from outside access by firewalls and access controls.

They are designed for secure, high speed, private collaboration and communication within an organization.

Intranets can include features such as:

  • Internal websites and portals
  • File sharing and collaboration tools
  • Employee directories and communication platforms
  • Access to internal databases and applications
